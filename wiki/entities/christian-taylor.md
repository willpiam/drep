# Christian Taylor

**Christian Taylor** (Open Source Cowboy / @deopensourceguy) is the founder and Chief Open Source Officer of **Open Source Cowboy Consulting**. He previously built Cardano's open source governance function at [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] and is the proposer of the **[[concepts/dospo-omf|dOSPO/OMF]]** treasury pilot ([[proposals/revised-cardano-dospo-and-omf-program-2026]]).

## Position

Taylor argues Cardano needs an independent operational layer — not another foundation committee — to sustain open source infrastructure using dependency data, transparent reporting, and replaceable administration. He left a stable role to pursue this model outside Intersect's institutional constraints.

Open Source Cowboy Consulting operates independently of IOG, the Cardano Foundation, and EMURGO, with no contractual ties to founding entities per [[sources/revised-cardano-dospo-and-omf-program-proposal]].

## Intersect history

At Intersect MBO, Taylor served as **Senior Manager of Open Source Governance** and **Head of OSPO**, where he:

- Designed Intersect's OSPO and contribution governance frameworks
- Developed the initial maintainer support architecture
- Shaped ecosystem policy that informed the **[[concepts/paid-open-source-model|Paid Open Source Model (POSM)]]**

He cites running Intersect open source operations with a **two-person team** for most of its existence as evidence the dOSPO pilot can execute lean.

POSM treasury funding flowed to **Intersect MBO**, not to Taylor personally or Open Source Cowboy Consulting ([[sources/revised-cardano-dospo-and-omf-program-proposal]]).

## dOSPO/OMF work

Co-authored **dOSPO v1.0** and **OMF v1.0** whitepapers (March 2026) with contributors from Bitergia, CHAOSS, Apache Software Foundation, Linux Foundation Decentralized Trust, Intersect, Andamio Platform, Modus Create, Leadingbit Solutions, and Chinstrap Community.
