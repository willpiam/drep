# Dano Finance

**Dano Finance** is a Cardano DeFi protocol team (with contributor **fallen-icarus**) operating lending, concentrated liquidity, oracle aggregation, and composable transaction flows. Dano proposes **[[proposals/global-order-book-defi-kernel|Global Order Book / DeFi Kernel]]** treasury funding.

## Position

In [[sources/global-order-book-connect-cardano-defi]], Dano Finance requests **₳3,333,000** to accelerate the **[[concepts/defi-kernel|DeFi Kernel]]** standard and deliver Spot Leverage Order Book, American Options Market-Making Pools, and a Composable DeFi Transaction Builder SDK.

Self-reported operating metrics at submission: approximately **$18M TVL** and **10,000+ on-chain transactions**. Dano's roadmap already targets composable primitives (synthetics, leverage, refinancing, single-transaction execution) aligned with order-book coordination.
