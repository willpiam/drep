# Tweag (Modus Create)

**Tweag by Modus Create** is an engineering firm that has worked on Cardano core infrastructure since **January 2018**, including consensus and ledger teams, **Ouroboros Genesis** implementation, and **[[concepts/peras|Peras]]** design contributions.

## Position

Tweag positions itself as the long-running engineering partner for Cardano L1 protocol delivery — complementary to [[entities/io-research|IO Research]]'s research program ([[concepts/cardano-vision|Cardano Vision]]) and distinct from IOG's broader engineering portfolio.
