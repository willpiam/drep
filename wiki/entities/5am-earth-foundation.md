# 5am.earth Foundation

The **5am.earth Foundation** is the neutral steward of an open, Cardano-anchored **[[concepts/agricultural-trust-layer|agricultural trust layer]]** for global supply chains. Commercial partners build application paths (certification, traceability, finance) on shared verified farmer/farm/crop infrastructure rather than each rebuilding onboarding and verification.

## Position

The Foundation's role is governance and stewardship of the trust layer — access rules, privacy, reporting, auditability, partner participation — not capture by a single commercial actor. Pre-Foundation co-promoters **Elk GMBH** and **HashPoint Consulting Sarl** (both Swiss-based) operate until legal registration at **M1 Stand-Up** (month 6 of the funded programme).

## Treasury proposal

[[proposals/5am-earth-trust-layer-vision-2030-kpis]] requests **₳10,000,000 (hard cap)** over 18 months, administered by [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] via TRSC/PSSC. HashPoint handles technical integration and project coordination.

## Live operational baseline

Scale-up builds on [[concepts/project-swaminathan|Project Swaminathan]] ([[entities/syngenta-foundation-india|Syngenta Foundation India]]), already on Cardano Mainnet with farmer SSI and on-chain farm records.

## Sustainability target

By **2028**, the Foundation aims to operate on commercial revenue and institutional backing — not returning to Treasury for operating costs. Long-term 2030 projections cite 3M farmers, 112.5M annual on-chain transactions, and $900M TVL.
