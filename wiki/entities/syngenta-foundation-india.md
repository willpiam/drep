# Syngenta Foundation India

**Syngenta Foundation India** operates **[[concepts/project-swaminathan|Project Swaminathan]]**, the field-distribution arm of the 5am.earth agricultural trust layer programme. It deploys Agri-Entrepreneurs (AEs) who onboard and support smallholder farmers.

## Scale

| Metric | Figure (proposal) |
|--------|-------------------|
| Agri-Entrepreneurs | 26,000+ |
| Farmers reached | 2.6M–2.7M |
| New farmers added | 400,000–500,000/year |

The **Global AE Academy** (co-founded with IFC / World Bank Group, SAFIA, and Corteva Agriscience) provides an institutional vehicle to scale the AE model across emerging markets, with a multi-country plan targeting **100 million farmers**.

## Cardano Mainnet operations

Project Swaminathan runs on Cardano Mainnet today:

- **10,500** cumulative farmer/farm registrations as of 5 May 2026 (~500/day)
- Six districts of Amravati, Maharashtra: Achalpur, Anjangaon Surji, Chandurbazar, Daryapur, Morshi, Katol
- Each farmer receives Veridian self-sovereign identity; each farm registration creates on-chain verified land/crop records
- Jan–Mar 2026 pilot: 1,056 farms, 100% blockchain success rate; scale-up from 1 April 2026
