# Wirex

**Wirex** is a regulated fintech and payments company operating as a **Visa Principal Member**. It provides digital-asset-linked payment cards, banking rails, and stablecoin infrastructure to retail and institutional users. The company claims 7M+ users, 1.5M+ issued cards, and $20B+ in cumulative transaction volume.

## Position

Wirex is the vendor for [[proposals/wirex-real-world-payments]], a **₳3,961,538** Cardano Treasury withdrawal ([[sources/withdraw-wirex-real-world-payments]]) submitted by [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] after passing the 2026 Intersect Budget Process Hydra vote (67% threshold).

The proposal delivers **open-source onchain payments infrastructure** for Cardano — smart contract settlement, account abstraction, batched transactions, wallet/API layers — while connecting to Wirex's regulated card-issuing and banking rails. A core outcome is **Visa card issuance linked to onchain Cardano balances**, enabling spend at global merchants.

Wirex had **not received Cardano Treasury funding** in the 24 months prior to submission.
