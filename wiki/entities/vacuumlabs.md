# VacuumLabs

**VacuumLabs** is a Cardano ecosystem engineering vendor associated with **hardware wallet interoperability** tooling — notably **cardano-hw-interop-library** and **cardano-hw-cli**, named in **CIP-21** ([[sources/cip-21-hardware-wallet-interoperability]]) as reference implementations for validating and transforming transactions into HW-compatible format.

## Position

VacuumLabs maintains the software layer between Cardano's full transaction model and Ledger/Trezor device constraints. Prior treasury and Catalyst funding cited in [[sources/withdraw-hardware-wallet-maintenance-2026]] includes:

- **Hardware Wallets Maintenance** (Intersect 2025 budget)
- **Ledger App Rewrite** (Intersect 2025 budget)
- **IO & VacuumLabs: Enhancing Plutus** (joint treasury withdrawal)
- Catalyst: Ledger Live integration, CIP-8/CIP-30 message signing for Trezor/Ledger, CTF and programmable-token tooling

The **[[proposals/hardware-wallet-maintenance-2026|2026 maintenance proposal]]** (₳1,310,960) is submitted by Intersect on behalf of an unnamed vendor; scope (Ledger/Trezor apps, cardano-hw-cli, interop library, integrator support) aligns with VacuumLabs' documented tooling footprint.

## Key deliverables (2026 proposal scope)

- Ledger and Trezor compatibility updates across protocol and vendor release cycles
- cardano-hw-cli and cardano-hw-interop-library maintenance
- Developer support for ecosystem integrators
- Vendor-required product or security audits when firmware changes trigger them
