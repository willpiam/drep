# Minswap Labs

**Minswap Labs** is a Cardano ecosystem team associated with the Minswap DEX. In treasury proposals it serves as an **established budget administrator** — fund administration, milestone review, and accountability — distinct from the SundaeSwap smart-contract escrow framework used by other 2026 asks.

## Position

In [[sources/global-order-book-connect-cardano-defi]], Minswap Labs administers **[[proposals/global-order-book-defi-kernel|Dano Finance's DeFi Kernel proposal]]** for a **1% budget administration fee** (₳33,000 on ₳3.3M delivery budget).

This is an **off-chain administrator role** (milestone review and disbursement process), not the on-chain TRSC/PSSC pattern used by Intersect-administered withdrawals.
