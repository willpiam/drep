# Cardano Foundation

The **Cardano Foundation** is one of Cardano's founding entities. It participates in Pentad governance structures (including [[concepts/cardano-critical-integrations|CCI]]) as a co-proposer and Steering Committee member, and serves on Intersect-administered **TRSC/PSSC Oversight Committees** as a trusted multisig entity.

## CCI / Pentad role

In [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v1|CCI V1]] and [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v2|CCI V2]], the Foundation is a co-proposer alongside IOG, EMURGO, Midnight Foundation, and Intersect (Administrator). The Pentad Steering Committee includes Foundation representation for maintenance prioritization and enhancement-reserve approvals.

## TRSC oversight role

Per [[concepts/treasury-escrow-oversight]], the Foundation co-signs selected on-chain TRSC/PSSC operations (e.g. 2-of-5 for Fund/Modify; 3-of-5 for Disperse/Reorganize) alongside Sundae Labs, Dquadrant, Xerberus, and NMKR. Purpose: verify administrative actions without governance discretion — not technical milestone acceptance or contracting.

## DRep considerations

Published vote context on CCI V2 ([vote 65](../../vote_context/markdown/65_critical.md)) records concern that the Foundation has **no community-elected board members**, cited as a factor in the **NO** vote on [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v2]].
