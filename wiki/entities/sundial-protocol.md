# Sundial Protocol

**Sundial Protocol** is a Cardano ecosystem team focused on Bitcoin-native product architecture, treasury accountability, institutional capital formation, and governance reporting. Sundial co-proposes **[[proposals/alchemy-cardano-native-bitcoin-treasury|Alchemy]]** with [[entities/charms|Charms]].

## Position

In [[sources/alchemy-sundial-charms-cardano-native-bitcoin-treasury-protocol]], Sundial leads protocol product design, go-to-market, governance reporting, and controlled deployment of treasury-linked funds. Sundial commits to disclose related-party relationships and material commercial arrangements with Charms or third parties.

Sundial also appears on Intersect's **2026 TRSC Oversight Committee** roster ([[sources/withdraw-hardware-wallet-maintenance-2026]]), replacing Xerberus.

## Proposal-linked role (Alchemy)

- Reserve architecture and FIRE/ICE economic design.
- Staged launch liquidity coordination and monthly governance reporting.
- Ecosystem education, DRep communications, and partner activation.
- Accountability for milestone-gated deployment and compliance with fund-use restrictions.
