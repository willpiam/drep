# Tastenkunst GmbH

**Tastenkunst GmbH** is the legal entity behind [[entities/eternl|Eternl]]. It submits treasury governance proposals, administers withdrawn funds for Eternl operational grants, and employs the Eternl development team (~10 contributors; **6.0 FTE** funded by the 2026–2027 proposal).

## Position

For [[proposals/eternl-path-to-sustainability-2026-2027]], Tastenkunst is both **submitting entity** and **treasury fund administrator** — a different pattern from [[entities/intersect|Intersect]]-administered TRSC/PSSC contracts used for IO Research and Tweag withdrawals.

Administrative model:

- **Non-milestone** 12-month operational funding (August 2026–July 2027)
- Full withdrawal converted to **stablecoins** in a **public company wallet**
- **[[concepts/treasury-payback-model|Payback assessments]]** every December and June
- Pro-plan purchases recorded **on-chain with metadata**; public earnings and repayment transaction reports

The 2025 Eternl treasury withdrawal (₳583,000) was administered by **Intersect**; the 2026–2027 proposal shifts administration to Tastenkunst directly.
