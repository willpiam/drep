# FluidTokens

**FluidTokens** is a Cardano and Bitcoin-focused DeFi builder and co-proposer of the Bifrost bridge program with [[entities/lantr-engineering|Lantr Engineering]].

## Position

In [[sources/bifrost-unlocking-bitcoin-defi-on-cardano-road-to-mainnet-phase-1-of-2]], FluidTokens is presented as a delivery team for Bitcoin-Cardano bridge infrastructure, with prior Catalyst-funded progress to a live public testnet. The proposal positions the team as technical vendor rather than long-term bridge owner, with ownership intended to transition to an independent stewardship structure before public launch.

## Proposal-linked capabilities (self-described)

- Cardano/Bitcoin DeFi product experience since 2022 (lending, renting, Bitcoin token flows, account abstraction).
- Smart-contract and full-stack integration capability across Cardano and Bitcoin.
- Audit history cited with Vacuumlabs, Anastasia Labs, and No.Witness Labs.
- Integration collaboration claims with Cardano dApps including Minswap and others.
