# Input Output Group (IOG)

**Input Output Group** (IOG, often “IO”) is the primary engineering and research company behind Cardano. It houses [[entities/io-research|IO Research]] (IOR), Applied Research & Creative Engineering (ARC), the Cardano Business Unit (CBU), and core node/smart-contract engineering.

## Governance relationship

Large treasury withdrawals are typically structured as legal contracts between **Input Output** and **Cardano Development Holdings (CDH)**, administered by [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] with milestone-based disbursement via treasury reserve smart contracts (TRSC/PSSC).
