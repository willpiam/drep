# Clarity

**Clarity** is the technical and business development team behind **[[concepts/initiative-dao|Initiative DAO]]** infrastructure on Cardano — smart contract-governed funding bodies where ecosystem participants coordinate treasury allocation, proposal review, and on-chain disbursement via [clarity.vote](https://www.clarity.vote).

## Position

Clarity builds and operates the tooling layer that enables DAOs like the **[[entities/cardano-builder-dao|Cardano Builder DAO]]** to run multi-cycle governance: membership units, governing documents, temperature checks, multi-output treasury withdrawals, and public governance portals.

For CB DAO, Clarity states it will **not** seek funding as a requesting member through any Initiative DAO it facilitates. The enabling tools and technology were funded by CB DAO in 2025.

## History

Clarity published reflections on CB DAO as its largest Initiative DAO deployment — two governance rounds, 11M+ ADA distributed to 34 companies, smart-contract-enforced treasury withdrawals, and iterative governance improvements (board accountability, KPI dashboards, conflict-of-interest rules).
