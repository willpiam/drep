# Midnight Foundation

The **Midnight Foundation** stewards the Midnight privacy sidechain ecosystem. It joined the Pentad structure for [[concepts/cardano-critical-integrations|Cardano Critical Integrations]] as a co-proposer and Steering Committee voting member alongside IOG, the Cardano Foundation, and EMURGO.

## CCI / Pentad role

In [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v1|CCI V1]] and [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v2|CCI V2]], Midnight Foundation is a co-proposer. The DRep YES rationale on V1 ([vote 29](../../vote_context/markdown/29_CriticalIntegrations.md)) noted Midnight can contribute substantially to integration goals.
