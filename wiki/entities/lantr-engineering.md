# Lantr Engineering

**Lantr Engineering** is a Cardano/Bitcoin infrastructure R&D company and maintainer of **[[concepts/scalus|Scalus]]** — JVM-native Cardano application development platform. Lantr co-leads Bifrost with [[entities/fluidtokens|FluidTokens]].

## Position

Lantr builds and maintains Scalus (smart contracts, transaction building, emulation, testing) used directly by Gummiworm L2, Bifrost, SugarRush, Vela, and DID/DIDComm, and indirectly via MeshJS, Lucid Evolution, Evolution SDK, Cardano Client Lib, and YaciDevKit.

In [[sources/bifrost-unlocking-bitcoin-defi-on-cardano-road-to-mainnet-phase-1-of-2]], Lantr is a treasury-withdrawal co-proposer for Bifrost Phase 1. In [[sources/scalus-2026-maintenance-dijkstra-readiness]], Lantr proposes a reduced **[[proposals/scalus-2026|Scalus 2026]]** continuation: maintenance, **[[concepts/dijkstra-hard-fork|Dijkstra]]** readiness, JVM/JS interoperability, and scoped application runtime.

Constitutional disclosure: second treasury withdrawal within 24 months (2025 Scalus DApps platform completed).

## Proposal-linked capabilities (self-described)

- Protocol and cryptography engineering for Bitcoin-Cardano bridge design.
- Infrastructure collaborations across Cardano tooling and interoperability efforts.
- Working-group participation and open-source contribution claims.
- Joint delivery capacity with FluidTokens to cover contracts, watchtowers, coordination software, SDK, and operations.
