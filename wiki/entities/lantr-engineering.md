# Lantr Engineering

**Lantr Engineering** is a Cardano/Bitcoin infrastructure R&D and engineering team co-leading Bifrost with [[entities/fluidtokens|FluidTokens]].

## Position

In [[sources/bifrost-unlocking-bitcoin-defi-on-cardano-road-to-mainnet-phase-1-of-2]], Lantr is a treasury-withdrawal proposer and delivery team for Bifrost Phase 1: security hardening, audits, private-mainnet launch readiness, and stewardship/economic setup.

The proposal includes constitutional disclosure that Lantr had a prior treasury withdrawal in the previous 24 months for Scalus-related work.

## Proposal-linked capabilities (self-described)

- Protocol and cryptography engineering for Bitcoin-Cardano bridge design.
- Infrastructure collaborations across Cardano tooling and interoperability efforts.
- Working-group participation and open-source contribution claims.
- Joint delivery capacity with FluidTokens to cover contracts, watchtowers, coordination software, SDK, and operations.
