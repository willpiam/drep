# EMURGO

**EMURGO** is one of Cardano's founding entities. It participates in Pentad governance structures (including [[concepts/cardano-critical-integrations|CCI]]) as a co-proposer and Steering Committee voting member alongside IOG, the Cardano Foundation, and Midnight Foundation.

## CCI / Pentad role

In [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v1|CCI V1]] and [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v2|CCI V2]], EMURGO is a co-proposer. The Pentad Steering Committee evaluates maintenance renewals, enhancement-reserve drawdowns, and working-group stewardship. Intersect acts as non-voting Administrator.

## DRep considerations

Published vote context on CCI V2 ([vote 65](../../vote_context/markdown/65_critical.md)) records concern that EMURGO **controls an unhealthy amount of voting power**, cited as a factor in the **NO** vote on [[proposals/cardano-critical-integrations-v2]].

*Note:* Vote context uses "Emergo" spelling; on-chain proposal metadata uses "EMURGO."
