# Pauli Group

**Pauli Group** is the author affiliation for Pierre-Luc Dallaire-Demers, William Doyle, and Timothy Foo in [[sources/brace-for-impact-ecdlp-challenges]], a paper proposing public ECDLP challenge benchmarks for tracking fault-tolerant quantum cryptanalysis.

## Position

The paper argues that transparent, reproducible benchmarks are needed before large fault-tolerant quantum computers become economically relevant. Its practical stance is proactive migration: digital assets should move toward post-quantum signatures before demonstrations reach serious ECDLP rungs.

## History

In the 2026 arXiv paper, Pauli Group authors introduce a secp256k1-shaped [[concepts/ecdlp-challenge-ladder]] and calibrate it against classical Pollard's rho records plus quantum resource estimates under surface-code and cat-code architectures.
