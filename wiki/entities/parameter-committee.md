# Parameter Committee (Intersect)

The **Parameter Committee** runs the **Parameter Change Process (PCP)** for Cardano protocol parameters. It provides technical review and recommendations before parameter-change governance actions are submitted on-chain.

## PCP-005: committeeMinSize

Reviewed the proposal to reduce [[concepts/committee-min-size|`committeeMinSize`]] from 7 to 5. Off-chain discussion: [PCP-005 on Cardano Forum](https://forum.cardano.org/t/pcp-005-committeeminsize-yuta/151156) (Yuta, **2025-11-11**). Recommended the change in support of [[proposals/committee-min-size-7-to-5]].

## Plutus cost models (van Rossem)

Recommended [[proposals/update-plutus-cost-models]] at triweekly meetings **2026-03-05** ([notes](https://forum.cardano.org/t/mar-5-2026-parameter-committee-triweekly-meeting-notes/154363)) and **2026-03-19** ([notes](https://forum.cardano.org/t/mar-19-2026-parameter-committee-triweekly-meeting-notes/154364)). The action updates [[concepts/plutus-cost-models|Plutus V1/V2/V3 cost models]] for new builtins (CIP-109, 132, 133, 138, 153), cross-version builtin parity, and re-benchmarked existing primitives ahead of the **[[concepts/van-rossem-hard-fork|van Rossem hard fork]]** (Protocol Version 11).

## Funding context (Intersect operations 2026-2027)
