# Cardano Vision 2026 (IO Research)

**Type:** Treasury withdrawal (research program)  
**Proposer:** [[entities/io-research|IO Research]] / [[entities/input-output-group|Input Output Group]]  
**Administrator:** [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] (on behalf of CDH)  
**Gov action:** [gov_action1ttgs45ulfxs0jwkfrecystc3flduhszmyzk8wnd7yw5za77tsg9qq4afmus](https://cardanoscan.io/govAction/gov_action1ttgs45ulfxs0jwkfrecystc3flduhszmyzk8wnd7yw5za77tsg9qq4afmus)  
**Source:** [[sources/cardano-vision-2026-ior]]

Cardano Vision 2026 is the second-year funding request for IO Research’s five-year **[[concepts/cardano-vision|Cardano Vision]]** program. The draft proposal (community review, May 2026) asks **₳32,916,667** (~$7.9M at ₳0.24/USD) for 36 FTE-years of research and technology validation organized into seven work packages, advancing Cardano toward 2030 objectives under the themes *human centred, scalable, and post quantum secure*.

## Work packages (summary)

| WP | Focus | Total FTE |
|----|--------|-----------|
| WP1 | Trust, security, reliability (consensus, PQ, node keys) | 8.3 |
| WP2 | Scalability & execution (fees, DA, sharding, P2P, ZK/L2) | 9.9 |
| WP3 | Developer platform & UX (Plutus, light clients, Babel) | 5.7 |
| WP4 | Applications & liquidity (bridges, swaps, agent chains) | 3.6 |
| WP5 | Economic systems (SPO incentives, multi-resource, metrics) | 3.7 |
| WP6 | Governance & identity | 2.5 |
| WP7 | Delivery & dissemination (in-kind via IOG) | — |

Several production paths are explicitly **out of scope** here and assigned to separate IO engineering/treasury proposals (e.g. Linear Leios hard-fork, Babel Fees MVP/CIP-118 delivery, IO L2 DA implementation, bridge build-outs).
