# Cardano Critical Integrations V1

**Type:** Treasury withdrawal (tier-one integration delivery)  
**Proposer:** Pentad ([[entities/input-output-group|IOG]], [[entities/cardano-foundation|Cardano Foundation]], [[entities/emurgo|EMURGO]], [[entities/midnight-foundation|Midnight Foundation]])  
**Administrator:** [[entities/intersect|Intersect]]  
**Concept:** [[concepts/cardano-critical-integrations]]

CCI V1 secured **₳70,000,000** over **24 months** for initial delivery and launch readiness of tier-one infrastructure: stablecoins (Circle USDCx), institutional custody/wallet infrastructure, on-chain analytics (Dune), cross-chain bridges (LayerZero), and pricing oracles (Pyth).

## Strategic framing (V1)

The budget info action argued Cardano's TVL (~$192M, Nov 2025) lagged ecosystems with standard DeFi primitives. Without tier-one stablecoins, custody, bridges, oracles, and analytics, Cardano risks falling further behind in dapp, liquidity, and institutional adoption — undermining the value of future protocol scaling ([[concepts/leios|Leios]], etc.).

Administration: Intersect milestone-based funding; confidentiality limited public budget granularity; unused funds returned at 24-month end. V1 noted TRSC framework might not be fully utilized due to partner NDA constraints on cost disclosure.

## DRep position

- Budget info action: **YES** ([vote 29](../../vote_context/markdown/29_CriticalIntegrations.md), Nov 2025) — supported Pentad involvement, Intersect's appropriate administrative role, and critical-infrastructure framing.
- Treasury withdrawal: **YES**, enacted (Dec 2025).
