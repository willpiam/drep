## Problem

Cardano does not support quantum secure accounts while the quantum computing industry is scaling toward large scale machines which may compromise elliptic curve cryptography by the end of the decade.

## Solution

A research paper containing a complete inventory of the quantum computing vulnerabilities of Cardano as well as an analysis of the mitigations. This paper will serve as an action plan after Voltaire.

### \[IMPACT\] Please describe your proposed solution.

We will research the performance trade-offs between approaches to quantum secure accounts, identify additional critical infrastructure that may need to be upgraded, and produce recommendations for how to proceed.

Cardano has a unique implementation which will need to be considered when upgrading its cryptographic primitives.

We will primarily consider various NIST approved schemes such as FALCON or CRYSTALS-DILITHIUM.

### \[IMPACT\] How does your proposed solution address the challenge and what benefits will this bring to the Cardano ecosystem?

Quantum secure accounts will increase confidence among institutional investors. They will also make Cardano the obvious choice for building wealth over long time periods.

Of course they will also keep Cardano open for business while chains with no governance system will fail to upgrade to post-quantum cryptography.

### \[IMPACT\] How do you intend to measure the success of your project?

We would consider this project successful if our report leads to quantum secure accounts being implemented in Cardano. Another metric for success would be getting more community members to take this problem seriously and the formation of a post-quantum upgrade task-force.

### \[IMPACT\] Please describe your plans to share the outputs and results of your project?

The primary output of this project is a paper which describes various approaches and considerations to account for when upgrading Cardano to post-quantum cryptography. This paper will be made freely available to all.

We also plan to post regular video updates detailing our progress. Frequent drafts will also be shared with the community.

### \[CAPABILITY/ FEASIBILITY\] What is your capability to deliver your project with high levels of trust and accountability?

Pauli Group is a self-funded company which has been focused on the problem of upgrading blockchains with post-quantum cryptography on the Ethereum side for over a year now.

In that year we have learnt a lot about the problem we are proposing to solve. Our [LAMB whitepaper](https://anchorwallet.ca/whitepaper/) outlines our approach for EVM blockchains. While that paper is not an appropriate solution for Cardano it is a demonstration of our understanding of the problem.

### \[CAPABILITY/ FEASIBILITY\] What are the main goals for the project and how will you validate if your approach is feasible?

1. Identify vulnerabilities - we will look at the full stack of Cardano and identify ways in which a quantum equipped adversary could wreak havo
2. Draft solutions by looking at the requirements of each component in the system and finding appropriate replacement
3. Identify consequences of drafted solutions - we will identify ways in which solutions impact user experience. Will a potential solution drive up gas costs? Or increase the burden on SPOs? Do UTxOs need to be managed in any particular way?
