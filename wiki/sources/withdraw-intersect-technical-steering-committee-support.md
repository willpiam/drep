## Summary

Treasury withdrawal submitted by **[[entities/intersect|Intersect]]** on behalf of the **[[entities/technical-steering-committee|Intersect Technical Steering Committee (TSC)]]** for **12 months** of technical-governance support. The ask is **1,193,000 ADA** (WP1 256,000; WP2 832,000; WP3 105,000), covering community technical coordination, protocol-governance specialist groups, and a pilot independent technical-review function launching in 2027.

The proposal positions the TSC as foundational governance infrastructure aligned across all five strategy pillars, with strongest ties to Pillar 1 (infrastructure reliability and evidence-based protocol settings) and Pillar 3 (DRep decision support through independent technical review). It states full expenditure accounting with return of unspent funds, includes independent assurance via Appold, and notes work-package execution is contingent on approval of the IntersectMBO budget proposal.

Administration follows the standard **2026 Intersect TRSC/PSSC** model using the Sundae Labs contract framework and six-member Oversight Committee roster.

## Key points

- **Budget:** WP1 256,000 + WP2 832,000 + WP3 105,000 = **1,193,000 ADA**.
- **WP1:** Community-facing technical coordination (event participation, commissioned technical reports).
- **WP2:** Protocol governance and evolution support (Parameter Committee, CIP editors, Hard Fork Working Group).
- **WP3:** Pilot independent technical review programme for core infrastructure proposals (2027 launch; conflict-of-interest controls and published recusals).
- **Intersections with separate budgets:** TSC-led technical workshops, Security Council, and Bug Bounty are funded outside this withdrawal.
- **Governance controls:** TREASURY-02a/NCL compliant at submission; TRSC/PSSC multisig administration and dashboard transparency.
- **Prior funding disclosure:** TSC itself reports no direct treasury funding in prior 24 months; Intersect and its committees disclose related prior withdrawals totaling 22,385,000 ADA across listed actions.
