# 5am.earth Trust Layer Targeting Vision 2030 KPIs

**Raw:** [../raw/5am.earth-Trust-Layer-Targeting-Vision-2030-KPIs.json](../raw/5am.earth-Trust-Layer-Targeting-Vision-2030-KPIs.json)  
**Ingested:** 2026-06-19

## Summary

[[entities/5am-earth-foundation|5am.earth Foundation]] requests **₳10,000,000 (hard cap)** over **18 months** to scale an open, Cardano-anchored **[[concepts/agricultural-trust-layer|agricultural trust layer]]** across India, Cambodia, and Kenya. The layer combines **Veridian** self-sovereign identity for farmers and Agri-Entrepreneurs (AEs) with a Cardano on-chain satellite oracle (DigiFarm imagery + attestations). The Foundation is neutral steward; commercial partners build application paths on top.

**Live baseline:** [[concepts/project-swaminathan|Project Swaminathan]] (operated by [[entities/syngenta-foundation-india|Syngenta Foundation India]]) already runs on Cardano Mainnet — **10,500** farmer/farm registrations as of 5 May 2026 at ~500/day across six Maharashtra districts, with 100% blockchain success rate in the Jan–Mar 2026 pilot.

**Administration:** [[entities/intersect|Intersect]] on standard terms via Sundae Labs **TRSC/PSSC** framework; delivery separated from administration per Constitution Article II §7.5. Oversight Committee: Sundae Labs, Cardano Foundation, Dquadrant, Xerberus, NMKR.

**Payment schedule:** ₳5M on approval / ₳2M at M1 / ₳3M at M2 (no M3 payment — M3 is self-sustaining evidence gate).

## Key points

- **Three-layer architecture:** source layer (AEs, farmers, satellite, credentials) → Cardano trust layer (verifiable claims, privacy, audit trail) → application layer (APIs, selective disclosure).
- **Funded application paths:** WP3 AE certification (**Andamio**); WP4 traceability/EUDR compliance (**Zengate Global**); WP5 finance/credit (**Seedstars SIGMA** — credit scoring, Cardano stablecoin-rail loans; Treasury funds rails, not loan capital).
- **Milestones:** M1 Stand-Up (month 6) — 200k farmers, AE credentialed, architecture locked; M2 Closed Loop (month 12) — 350k farmers, three paths operating, first stablecoin loans; M3 Self-Sustaining (month 18) — 500k farmers, revenue model active, final audit.
- **Funded-period KPIs (M3):** 500k registered farmers; 2M on-chain transactions; $2M lending via Cardano stablecoin rails.
- **2030 projections:** 3M farmers; 112.5M annual on-chain transactions; $900M TVL; 16–20M ADA annual protocol revenue. Self-sustaining by 2028 via commercial partnerships — no further Treasury operating costs.
- **Technical cohort (operational today):** AIQuant/Anastasia Labs smart contracts; DigiFarm satellite oracle; Veridian DID; Andamio credentials; NMKR tokenisation.
- **Field scale:** Syngenta Foundation India — 26k+ AEs reaching 2.6M+ farmers; Global AE Academy (IFC/World Bank, SAFIA, Corteva) targeting 100M farmers.
- **Prior Catalyst funding:** Project Swaminathan Catalyst Fund 13 (project ID 1300178) — no prior Article II §7 treasury withdrawal in 24 months for consortium entities.
- **Constitutional compliance:** Article II §3(2) disclosure — Zengate Global operates an active SPO (no §3(2) voting conflict for treasury withdrawals). Hard cap with $3.5M scope-elasticity; surplus above returns to Treasury. Refund conditions for unspent ADA, milestone failure, fraud, scope abandonment.
- **Reporting:** public on-chain KPI dashboard (monthly); quarterly narrative reports; monthly Cardano Forum updates; independent audits at M2 and M3.
