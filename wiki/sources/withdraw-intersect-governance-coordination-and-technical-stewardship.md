## Summary

Treasury withdrawal submitted by **[[entities/intersect|Intersect]]** to fund its core operating model from June 2026 to June 2027. The ask is **25,400,000 ADA** across governance coordination, technical stewardship of core Cardano infrastructure, and reserved capacity for critical unowned processes.

The largest component is **WP2 (18,800,000 ADA)** for technical stewardship: upgrade/release coordination, incident response, repository stewardship for core Haskell code, and security initiatives (including bug bounty coordination). **WP1 (6,000,000 ADA)** funds Intersect operations and ecosystem/governance coordination, while **WP3 (600,000 ADA)** reserves capacity for critical processes that otherwise lack ownership.

The proposal frames this as foundational operational infrastructure for decentralized governance and network resilience, with independent assurance by Appold and milestone-based drawdowns through the 2026 Intersect TRSC/PSSC model.

## Key points

- **Budget:** WP1 6,000,000 + WP2 18,800,000 + WP3 600,000 = **25,400,000 ADA**.
- **What is funded:** governance coordination, committee/workgroup support, technical stewardship, incident response, release coordination, repository management, and critical-process coverage.
- **What is not specified:** no per-person roster or explicit named compensation schedule in metadata.
- **Intersect claim:** ask reduced year-over-year ($7.875M to $6.35M equivalent) while preserving critical continuity functions.
- **Controls:** TREASURY-02a / 350M NCL compliant at submission; milestone reporting and assurance.
- **Administration:** standard 2026 TRSC/PSSC with six-member Oversight Committee.
