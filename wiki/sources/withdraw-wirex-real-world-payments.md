## Summary

Treasury withdrawal submitted by **[[entities/intersect|Intersect]]** on behalf of **[[entities/wirex|Wirex]]**, advancing a **2026 Intersect Budget Process** proposal that passed Hydra voting. The ask is **₳3,961,538** for **WP1 — Enabling Onchain Payments & Card Infrastructure**: full-stack, open-source payments infrastructure connecting Cardano to banking rails, stablecoin systems, and wallet interfaces, with **Visa card issuance** linked to onchain balances.

Wirex is a regulated payments business (7M+ users, 1.5M+ cards, $20B+ transaction volume, Visa Principal Member). The proposal frames Cardano as lacking integrated real-world payment rails despite strong DeFi foundations. All deliverables are released as public goods (smart contracts, account abstraction, batched transactions, settlement logic, wallet/API layer).

Strategic alignment spans **Pillar 1** (open-source on-chain infrastructure) and **Pillar 2** (Payments as an identified high-value vertical). Funds flow through Intersect's **2026 TRSC/PSSC** ([[entities/sundae-labs|Sundae Labs]] framework) with the standard six-member Oversight Committee.

- **Budget:** WP1 ₳3,846,153 + Intersect admin ₳115,385 = **₳3,961,538** total.

- **Technical scope:** Smart contract settlement, account abstraction, batched transactions, settlement logic, wallet and API ecosystem layer — all open source.
- **Admin model:** Intersect TRSC → PSSC; 2026 TRSC stake `stake1784sdxt6jjennmstphgdu7l7c2scf5d02a6cve2dgn5s2kq5u3j9v`.

- **NCL:** Compliant with TREASURY-02a at submission (350M NCL, epochs 613–713).
