## Summary

Treasury withdrawal from **[[entities/intersect|Intersect]]** (co-proposed with [[entities/input-output-group|IOG]], [[entities/cardano-foundation|Cardano Foundation]], [[entities/emurgo|EMURGO]], and [[entities/midnight-foundation|Midnight Foundation]]) requesting **₳23,000,000** (~$5.75M at $0.25/ADA) for a **12-month Year 2 maintenance and enhancement program** under the [[concepts/cardano-critical-integrations|Cardano Critical Integrations (CCI)]] initiative.

CCI V2 sustains and optimizes infrastructure delivered under **CCI V1** (₳70M, enacted Dec 2025): **Circle USDCx**, **LayerZero** cross-chain messaging/bridge, **Pyth** price feeds, and **Dune Analytics** coverage. V2 adds a new **Fireblocks** native institutional custody integration. Funds are administered by Intersect via the Treasury Reserve Smart Contract Framework; the **Pentad Steering Committee** (IOG, CF, EMURGO, Midnight + Intersect as non-voting Administrator) approves maintenance renewals and enhancement-reserve drawdowns (3/4 quorum).

The proposal discloses prior treasury receipt of ₳70M under CCI V1 in the prior 24 months (Constitution Article II.7.2). Vendor-level costs are confidential; public breakdown is by category only. Unused funds return to treasury at period end.

## Key points

- **Budget (₳23M):** Integration & maintenance ~90% (₳20.7M); enhancement & tooling reserve ~5% (₳1.15M, returned if unused); legal/audit/admin ~5% (₳1.15M).
- **Scope:** Year 2 licensing/platform fees for V1 integrations; Cardano-side ops (attestors, DVN/endpoints, oracle feeds, Dune subgraphs); Fireblocks Year 1 integration + platform fee; ring-fenced enhancement reserve for SDKs, monitoring, reconciliation tooling.
- **Exclusions:** New tier-1 integrations (USDT0, LayerZero OFT expansion, other custodial/analytics targets); dapp funding; liquidity programs; marketing/events — each to be separate governance actions.
- **Administration:** Intersect TRSC/PSSC; multisig oversight from Sundae Labs, Cardano Foundation, DQuadrant, Xerberus, NMKR; auto-abstain delegation, no SPO staking.
- **Reporting:** Bi-annual reports on financial reconciliation, maintenance status per integration, Fireblocks progress, enhancement drawdowns, audits, and future governance-action slate.
- **Urgency framing:** V1 contracts expire or need renewal within the 12-month window; without V2, each maintenance line would require its own treasury action.
- **Recurring-cost nature:** Proposal explicitly frames annual licensing/subscription costs as baseline operating expense for tier-one infrastructure.
