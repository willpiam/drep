## Summary

Treasury withdrawal submitted by **[[entities/intersect|Intersect]]** on behalf of an unnamed vendor, advancing a **2026 Intersect Budget Process** proposal that passed Hydra voting. The ask is **₳1,310,960** for **12 months** of production maintenance of Cardano **hardware-wallet** compatibility — Ledger and Trezor firmware/app updates, **cardano-hw-cli** and **cardano-hw-interop-library** upkeep, integrator developer support, and vendor-required security audits when firmware changes demand them.

This is a **continuity** proposal for an established access layer, not a new wallet product. Scope excludes broader core-node, ledger, or general infrastructure maintenance. Primary Cardano strategy alignment is **Pillar 1** (secure, interoperable signing); secondary **Pillar 2** (adoption via secure UX and developer tooling).

Funds flow through Intersect's **2026 TRSC/PSSC** (Sundae Labs framework) with milestone metadata, capped T&M reporting, and the updated six-member Oversight Committee (includes **[[entities/eternl|Eternl]]** and Sundial, replacing Xerberus from the 2025 roster).

- **Budget:** WP1 ₳1,272,777 + Intersect admin ₳38,183 = **₳1,310,960** total.
- **Duration:** 12 months (2026).
- **Deliverables:** Ledger/Trezor compatibility; cardano-hw-cli and interop library maintenance; ecosystem integrator support; audits triggered by vendor firmware changes.
- **Admin model:** Intersect TRSC → PSSC; Sundae Labs smart contracts; 2026 TRSC stake `stake1784sdxt6jjennmstphgdu7l7c2scf5d02a6cve2dgn5s2kq5u3j9v`.
- **Oversight Committee (2026):** Sundae Labs, Cardano Foundation, Dquadrant, NMKR, Sundial, Eternl — verify admin actions on-chain without governance discretion.
- **NCL:** Compliant with TREASURY-02a at submission (350M NCL, epochs 613–713).
- **Prior funding cited:** Hardware Wallets Maintenance and Ledger App Rewrite (Intersect budget 2025); IO & VacuumLabs Plutus enhancement; multiple Catalyst projects for Ledger Live, message signing, CTF, programmable tokens.
- **Interop context:** Maintenance directly supports **[[concepts/hardware-wallet-interoperability|CIP-21]]** constraints that software wallets and tools must satisfy for Ledger/Trezor signing.
