# Bifrost: Unlocking Bitcoin DeFi on Cardano — Road to Mainnet (Phase 1 of 2)

**Raw:** [../raw/Bifrost_-Unlocking-Bitcoin-DeFi-on-Cardano-—-Road-to-Mainnet-(Phase-1-of-2).json](../raw/Bifrost_-Unlocking-Bitcoin-DeFi-on-Cardano-—-Road-to-Mainnet-(Phase-1-of-2).json)  
**Ingested:** 2026-07-08

## Summary

Treasury-withdrawal proposal by **[[entities/fluidtokens|FluidTokens]]** and **[[entities/lantr-engineering|Lantr Engineering]]** requesting **₳12,332,031** (about $1.97M at $0.16/ADA) for a 9-month **Phase 1** program (Jul 2026 to Mar 2027) to move Bifrost from a Catalyst-funded testnet bridge to an audited private mainnet deployment.

The proposal positions Bitcoin liquidity as Cardano's largest near-term DeFi expansion opportunity and frames Bifrost as a **permissionless Bitcoin-Cardano bridge** secured by Cardano SPOs (threshold signing) with a federated fallback mode. Phase 1 funds hardening, external audits, formal verification, ecosystem-readiness work, and legal/stewardship/economic setup. Public launch plus 24-month operations are explicitly deferred to a separate Phase 2 proposal in Q1 2027.

## Key points

- **Total ask:** ₳12,332,031 (includes 10% refundable contingency); Phase 2 funded separately.
- **Core objective:** audited bridge on Cardano mainnet under controlled private access, proven in both custody modes before public rollout.
- **Security scope:** architecture review, external audits (contracts/crypto/off-chain), formal verification, pentest, bug bounty.
- **Bridge model:** BTC custody via Cardano SPO threshold signatures (FROST/Taproot), watchtowers, plus federated continuity fallback.
- **Asset model:** fBTC is represented as a native Cardano asset composable across trading, lending, and collateral use cases.
- **Governance/funding controls:** milestone-gated treasury-contract escrow, independent oversight board, independent technical and financial audits, auto-abstain DRep delegation and no SPO delegation on escrowed funds.
- **Ecosystem claims:** named SPO test participants and dApp integration interest; proposal targets Cardano 2030 KPIs via BTC liquidity inflow.
