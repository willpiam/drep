# Ecosystem KPI Funding

**Ecosystem KPI funding** is the treasury pattern of tying withdrawals and sub-grants to measurable Cardano adoption metrics — especially **monthly active users (MAU)**, **monthly on-chain transactions**, and **total value locked (TVL)** — aligned with the **[[concepts/cardano-vision|Vision 2030]]** strategy framework.

## Position

As Cardano moves toward Vision 2030, proposals increasingly argue treasury capital should connect to verifiable outcomes rather than narrative reporting alone. Implementation spans:

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Proposal-level KPI claims | [[proposals/5am-earth-trust-layer-vision-2030-kpis]], [[proposals/io-hydra]], [[proposals/blockfrost-transformation-to-not-for-profit]] |
| Sub-grant KPI enforcement | [[entities/cardano-builder-dao|Cardano Builder DAO]] — funded members report MAU, transactions, TVL |
| Dashboard evolution | CB DAO KPI dashboard v1 (self-reported) → v2 (live on-chain Cardano data) |

## CB DAO model

The **[[entities/cardano-builder-dao|Cardano Builder DAO]]** is the most developed **[[concepts/initiative-dao|Initiative DAO]]** example: builders fund builders, require KPI tracking from recipients, and position the DAO as an oversight body coordinating KPI-based funding with accountability standards. The DAO explicitly offers to work with dReps on standardized KPI tracking cadence.
