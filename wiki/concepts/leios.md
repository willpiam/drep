# Leios (Linear Leios)

**Leios** (including **Linear Leios** as the next-generation throughput upgrade) is Cardano’s research and engineering track to increase L1 throughput by optimizing block propagation and network efficiency while preserving Ouroboros security guarantees.

## Research vs engineering split

Cardano Vision 2026 ([[sources/cardano-vision-2026-ior]]) funds **IOR** work on:

- MEV vectors and mempool partitioning under Leios
- Adversarial and degraded-mode validation; SPO operational boundaries
- Peras–Leios interaction and certificate optimisation
- Formal reports and protocol improvement papers

**Out of scope for IOR:** Productionisation in the **IO Consensus Initiative**—hard-fork preparation, parameter validation, large-scale testnet, red-teaming, production threat modelling.

## Status (as of 2025 delivery, per proposal)

- Prototype progressed; handed to engineering with a **CIP**
- Deployment readiness targeted **2026** alongside Peras
- Cited KPI linkage: 3× throughput capacity; path toward 27M monthly transactions
