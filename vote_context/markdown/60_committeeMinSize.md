## Statement

I am voting against this action to reduce the committeeMinSize from 7 to 5. We should be looking to grow this committee. If we can't find 7 people to do this job we should probably take a moment as a community to figure out what we are getting wrong before we move forward with anything else.

It would not take much money to make the position desirable to a much wider group. Even $12k per year would be enough for someone in my position to justify that time investment to my family. I would love to do it but there's only so much time I can allocate to volunteer efforts.

We had an opportunity to expand the committee size back in December [1] but the community voted not to move forward with it. I don't know how we managed to screw that up. 

Just today I saw Big Pey is leaving his position as a dRep. He said it's not worth his time because of *constant stress and harassment*[2]. We seem to be tolerating an unacceptable level of poor behaviour within our ecosystem. I encourage the community to consider putting together a legal fund to go after Cardano community members committing harassment.
