## Summery

- Proposes a 50M ada budget over 12 months to supply stablecoin liquidity
- 9 member administrating committee
- Each member is paid 1K ada/month
- 108K ada paid to administrators (in a year)
- 500K ada for 
    - Smart contract setup (~225K ada)
    - Smart contract audit (75K ada)
    - Legal fees (200K ada)
- Funds may only contain: 
    - ADA
    - fiat-backed stablecoins
    - liquidity tokens
- committee will create a report each month about the deployed fund
- once a month 15% of revenue will be converted to ADA and sent back to the treasury while 85% will remain in protocols to continue growing
- 5 of 9 signature required to spend funds
- tDAO
    - can turn off committee actions
    - can remove and elect committee members
    - can shut everything down
    - requires at least 25% of dRep participation
    - 51% of participating dreps must agree for tDAO actions to be enacted

## Conclusion

Stablecoin liquidity may be the number one thing holding Cardano back right now. The solution proposed in this budget strikes a good balance between decentralized control and autonomous, rapid execution. With this in mind, I am voting in support of this budget.
